## Getting Started

AlertyAI is designed to be frictionless. Here's how you can start managing your tasks efficiently directly from your Android device.

### 1. Installation & Login
After installing the **AlertyAI Android App**, open it and you will be greeted with a secure, one-tap Google Sign-In powered by Android's native Credential Manager. 

### 2. Creating Tasks Your Way
AlertyAI offers multiple intelligent ways to create tasks, eliminating manual data entry:

#### 🎙 Voice-to-Task
Tap the microphone icon on the home screen and speak naturally. For example, say: _"Remind me to call John tomorrow at 3 PM about the marketing report."_ 
The AI will transcribe your voice and automatically extract the title, date, and time.

#### 📸 Image OCR & Parsing
Have a flyer, a meeting agenda, or a whiteboard with action items? 
Snap a photo or upload a screenshot. AlertyAI uses advanced OCR to read the text and actionable tasks are extracted instantly by our intelligent backend.

#### 💬 Smart Chat
Don't want to use voice? Just chat with the built-in AI assistant. Type, _"Schedule a team meeting on Friday at 5 PM,"_ and the AI will craft the task for you, properly categorized and scheduled.

#### ⌨️ Manual Entry
You can always manually create tasks. Enjoy native Android date and time pickers to handle detailed scheduling.

### 3. Setting Smart Alarms
Never miss a deadline. When a task has a specific time, you can configure smart alarms:
- **At time of event**
- **10 minutes before**
- **30 minutes before**
- **1 hour before**

### 4. Managing Your Day
The dashboard gives you a clear overview of your day. You can:
- Mark tasks as complete with a satisfying swipe or tap.
- Break large goals into smaller **Subtasks** and track their progress.
- Filter tasks by date or category natively within the app.
