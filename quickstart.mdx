## Before you begin

- Android phone (recommended Android 10+)
- Google account for sign-in
- Internet connection for first sync

## Step 1: Install AlertyAI

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="download" title="Download and install APK">
    Use the official download link from the AlertyAI website.
    Install the APK and allow required permissions when prompted.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="user-check" title="Sign in securely">
    Open the app and complete Google sign-in.
    This creates your account and enables sync-backed workflows.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Step 2: Create your first task

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="microphone" title="Use voice capture">
    Tap the mic button and speak naturally.
    Example: "Remind me to send the investor update tomorrow at 9 AM."
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="image" title="Use image-to-task">
    Upload a screenshot or photo (meeting notes, whiteboard, checklist).
    AlertyAI extracts actionable tasks from visible text.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Step 3: Set reminders and complete

<Accordion icon="bell" title="Configure reminder timing">
  Choose one reminder offset:
  - At event time
  - 10 minutes before
  - 30 minutes before
  - 1 hour before
</Accordion>
